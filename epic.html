Epic

PREVIOUS    |    NEXT

Epic is a music app specifically for the orchestral/movie soundtrack/classical/etc music genres. I love this kind of music, and most music apps don't cater to it very much. I wanted to create a simple app where you could easily access things you've saved, different subgenres, and even orchestral/classical music events.

Backstory to this project: I love the Chronicles of Narnia series by C.S. Lewis, and also stories about traveling to other worlds in general. In The Chronicles of Narnia: The Magician's Nephew, Lewis tells of how Digory Kirke and Polly Plummer, the main characters, ended up in Narnia. They acquired rings that took them to these different pools, and the pools took them to different worlds. I thought it would be fun to create a website based on the idea that there are pools like this scattered throughout the world, backed by an old secret society that provides resources and information about it. ... I am obviously a nerd.
